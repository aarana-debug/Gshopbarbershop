Our Story

G's Shop Barber Shop has been a cornerstone of the Pomona community for over a decade. Nestled at 317 E Foothill Blvd, we're more than just a place to get a haircut — we're where Pomona comes to look sharp, feel confident, and leave feeling like themselves.

From skin fades to classic cuts, beard sculpts to senior styles, our barbers bring precision, care, and years of expertise to every single chair. Walk in or book ahead — you're always welcome here.
